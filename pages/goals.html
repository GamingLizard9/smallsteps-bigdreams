Our Goals


In our Montessori learning program, we strive to instill many important concepts in your child, such as:


  • Greater understanding of the Christian faith
  • Development of large motor skills
  • Development of fine motor skills
  • Concentration
  • Coordination
  • Order
  • Confidence
  • Independence
  • Awareness of themselves and others
  • Creativity
  • Responsibilities for their surroundings (including care of the earth)
  • A love of learning
  • A confident child prepared for 1st grade
